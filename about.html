ABOUT

Pokhariya Secondary School is full fledged co-educational secondary school nestled in a heart of Biratnagar city in the district of Morang, Nepal . It was established in the year 2016 BS by Mr. Khemraj Bhattarai . A wide range of academic and other opportunities are offered to help confidence and self-esteem and to respect others to gain a good education and to acquire qualities necessary for various stages of pupils lives.

MISSION

We aim to provide a congenial learning environment by focusing on developing the emotional, social, physical, creative and spiritual potential of every child, who will contribute in the development of the nation, in future . "We at mascot make an effort to impart education through different innovative methods combined with modern techniques and expertise."

VISION

Our vision at Pokhariya is to empower students to acquire, demonstrate, articulate and value knowledge and skills that will support them, as life-long learners, to participate in and contribute to the global world and practice the core values of the school: respect, tolerance & inclusion, and excellence.
